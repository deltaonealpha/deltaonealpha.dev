PHMS

The Patient Health Monitoring System is a framework capable of returning ECG scans, monitoring pulse, temperature, motion and autonomously handling a patient's glucose drip, all while up-linking data to a common dashboard monitored by doctors in real-time. Machine learning instruction-set lets PHMS alert doctors of irregular patterns.

DELTA-NLP

DELTA-NLP is an advanced under-development library of literary constructors and de-constructors, which can breakdown sentences and also create meaningful sentences from random word dumps. It can identify emotions and word types to lay out the perfect sentence. DELTA-NLP paves the way for more advanced open-source conversational machines.

VISIONFLOW

Visionflow2 is a pair of spectacles with sensory equipment embedded in its polycarbonate frame, offering navigation by the means of haptics for patients with vision troubles or acute spondylosis. It implements a split-PCB model across both sides of the frame with effortless and wireless inter-rim communication.
Visionflow has won multiple awards at national level events and exhibitions.

More about Visionflow2
DVALVE

DVALVE2 is a tap attachment for urban homes, complete with sensors and logic cores, helping save water by automatically terminating water flow when the bucket placed underneath gets filled. DVALVE2 uses solenoids and miniature pumps to achieve this, controlled by a central microcontroller. DVALVE2 also has IoT connected capabilities.

More about DVALVE2
DBFA MANAGER

DBFA is a fully complete, advanced store manager written in Python. It takes care of everything revolving a store, from inventory to billing, from earnings to statistics.
Additionally, it comes loaded with DBFA Employee Manager, a way to manage, schedule and pay your employees.
