Lirecent

Lirecent is a reading platform for all and sundry, backed by privacy, driven by smarts. 
It offers well-composed literature from amateur and experienced writers alike, backed by our extensive recommendation and analysis system which ensures you get content tailor-fit to you, every time. Lirecent also has Food Blocks, a way for our users to create and share recipes with the world!

PHMS

The Patient Health Monitoring System framework is capable of monitoring real-time vitals, such as ECG scans, pulse, temperature, motion and more. It can also autonomously handle physical meters such as glucose drips et cetera.

All data is linked to the cloud, where its analyzed and stored. Doctors are immediately notified if the system observes changes in the patient's vitals.

Liquid Vision

Liquid Vision provides haptic-based navigation for people with ailments relating to vision and movement, built into one's glasses. A custom-built PCB is embedded in its polycarbonate frame by implementing a split-PCB model across both sides of the frame with effortless and wireless inter-rim communication.

Visionflow has won multiple awards at national level events and exhibitions.

Agrotes

The Agrotes is a fully autonomous, scalable, always-learning stowable plantation pod for use in outer-space and on terrain.

It has been designed to work as self-independently as possible, with minimum user interaction required. It automatically handles environment control, nutrient delivery, air pressure, lighting and everything required while learning to get better and better each time with its advanced neural network.
