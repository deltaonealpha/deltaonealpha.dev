Projects

Aileron Operations Command

Class-leading squadron flight panel software with path and function predictive capabilities.

Integrated fleet and mission control, analysis and charting platform that automates complex workflows, letting you sustain better and respond faster.

Predictive technologies let the Aileron fleet of vessels automatically think ahead of time and space, letting them be proactive and optimize travel through space as a path function.

Status: Currently in-progress

Lirecent CDP

A complete suite for content teams to chart, draft and collaborate over, topped with distribution management built-in.

CDP takes the concept of collaborative work to the next-level, bringing all that can be done together in an offline environment, to the new world of online work.

Create and collaborate over drafts, notebooks and media, conduct meetings and take textual and voice notes, distribute, earn, analyze and improve - all over CDP.

Status: Currently on-hold | Awaiting funding rounds

read.lirecent

Developed Lirecent Read, a next-gen open-source literature platform with a striking design experience.

Lirecent Read is my vision for the future of reading, with a striking user experience that keeps one gripped, using ample amounts of whitespace to deliver a clear yet clean and elegant interface.

Status: Fully-developed , discontinued pre-launch

Vision-flow

Haptic-based navigation for people with ailments relating to vision and movement, built into a sleek pair of glasses.

A custom-built PCB, embedded in a polycarbonate frame lets us achieve this functionality by using a split-PCB model across both sides of the frame.

Visionflow has won multiple awards at national level events and exhibitions.

Status: Discontinued after initial prototyping

Agrotes

Fully self-sustaining food production pods for long-haul extraterrestrial operations and settlements.

Agrotes is an advanced, scalable and self-sustaining space food system. Able to run both in-flight and on-terrain, it aims to provide its users with nutrients in fresh form. Also on-board are artificial gravity and pressurization systems that orient and guide plant growth in the absence of natural systems.

Status: Completed design and research on-paper
