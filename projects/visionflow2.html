PROJECTS // VisionFlow2

Visionflow2 is a pair of spectacles with sensory equipment embedded in its polycarbonate frame, offering navigation by the means of haptics for patients with vision troubles or acute spondylosis. It implements a split-PCB model across both sides of the frame with effortless and wireless inter-rim communication.
Visionflow has won multiple awards at national level events and exhibitions.
