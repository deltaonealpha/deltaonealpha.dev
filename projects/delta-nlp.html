PROJECTS // DELTA-NLP

DELTA-NLP is an advanced under-development library of literary constructors and de-constructors, which can breakdown sentences and also create meaningful sentences from random word dumps. It can identify emotions and word types to lay out the perfect sentence. DELTA-NLP paves the way for more advanced open-source conversational machines.
